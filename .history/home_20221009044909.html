Our Services

What We Do?

We are so passionate about offering the best and fast services to our customers,basically,trying so hard in giving our clients easy time and allow them book for their inspection dates and location at their own pleasure.

Insurance validation

Under Insurance validation,we check on the validity of the vehicle insurance,on the check list we look into:
1.The insurance company
2. The expirly date of the Insurance

Speed limiter validation

Speed governors is one of the key point of inspection because most accidents are caused by overspeeding.We look into:
1. The fitting company of the governor
2. The governor should be a digital one.
3.The expirly dates of the govenor.
4.The speed governor sticker and certificate.

Tyres and wheels

During inspection the tyres should be checked.We look into:
1.Wheels and fasteners fitted tightly.
2.Tyres in good physical shape.
3.Tyres properly inflated.
4. Power brakes in good condition.

Body and extrenal fittings

The physical body of the vehicle should be checked correctly.Considering:
1.wipers,headlights,sidemirrors and other mirrors should be in good condition.
2.Door locks and belts in goood shape
3.Exhaust system in good shape.
4.Free from excessive physical damage.
5. Vehicle body paint

Engine compartments

Engine checklist during inspection:
1.Engine oil level.
2. Brake fluid level.
3. Batterly secured.
4. Coolant level.
